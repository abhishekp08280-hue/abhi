Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Simply click on the "Register" button in the top navigation, fill out the registration form with your details, and verify your email address with the OTP we send you.

Is the platform free to use?

Yes, our basic features are completely free for both teachers and institutions. We may offer premium features in the future, but the core functionality will always remain free.

How do I apply for jobs?

Browse through available job postings, click on a job that interests you, and use the "Apply Now" button. Make sure your profile and resume are up to date before applying.

Can I upload study materials?

Absolutely! Teachers can upload and share study materials, lesson plans, and educational resources with the community. This helps build a collaborative learning environment.

How do live sessions work?

Live sessions are conducted using integrated video conferencing tools. Teachers can host sessions, and students can join using the provided meeting links. All sessions are recorded for future reference.
